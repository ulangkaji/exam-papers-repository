Workings for Q1

  • (W1) Plant & Radar:
    • Cost = RM2,200,000. Annual Dep = $\frac{2,200,000 - 40,000}{15} = 144,000$ (12,000/month).
    • Value Brought In (1 May 2024): Depreciated for 18 months (Nov 22 - Apr 24).
      $2,200,000 - (18 \times 12,000) = \text{RM1,984,000}$.
    • Value c/d (31 Dec 2024): Depreciated for 8 months (May - Dec).
      $1,984,000 - (8 \times 12,000) = \text{RM1,888,000}$.
  • (W2) Overheads: 1.5% of materials used.
    Materials Used = $(1,000,000 + 480,000) - 18,000 (\text{Ret}) - 12,000 (\text{End}) = 1,450,000$.
    Overheads = $1.5\% \times 1,450,000 = \text{RM21,750}$.
  • (W3) Revenue (Input Method):
    • Cost to Date = RM2,781,800.
    • Est. Total Cost = $2,781,800 + 1,471,600 + 95,000 = 4,348,400$.
    • % Completion = $2,781,800 / 4,348,400 = 63.97\%$.
    • Revenue = $63.97\% \times 6,000,000 \approx \text{RM3,838,380}$.

Workings for Q3

  • Variable Cost/Unit: $20+8+10+5 = \text{RM43}$.
  • Fixed OAR: $\text{RM63,000} / 12 / 750 = \text{RM7/unit}$.
  • Absorption Cost/Unit: $43 + 7 = \text{RM50}$.
  • Op Stock (Dec): Nov Prod (1,200) - Sales (675) = 525 units.
  • Over-absorption: Absorbed ($900 \times 7 = 6,300$) - Actual ($5,250$) = RM1,050 Over.

b. Reconciliation

Marginal Profit (36,300) - Absorption Profit (33,150) = RM3,150.
Difference = Change in Stock ($525 - 75 = 450 \text{ decrease}$) $\times$ Fixed OAR ($7$) = RM3,150.
