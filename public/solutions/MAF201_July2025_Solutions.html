Workings for Q1

  • (W1) Plant c/d: Cost RM200,000. Useful life 4 years. Duration 6 months (Oct-Mar).
    $$ \text{Depreciation} = \frac{200,000}{4} \times \frac{6}{12} = 25,000 $$
    Plant c/d = Sent Value (175,000) - Depreciation (25,000) = RM150,000.
  • (W2) Hire of Equipment: Paid RM15,000 for 15 months. Incurred for 6 months.
    $$ \text{Expense} = 15,000 \times \frac{6}{15} = 6,000 $$
    Prepaid = 15,000 - 6,000 = RM9,000.
  • (W3) HO Overheads: 20% of Materials Used.
    Materials Used = Purchases (162,000) - Closing Stock (25,000) = 137,000.
    Overhead = 20% × 137,000 = RM27,400.
  • (W4) Revenue (Input Method):
    Est. Total Contract Cost (ETCC) = Cost to Date (316,650) + Future Costs (211,100) = 527,750.
    % Completion = $$ \frac{316,650}{527,750} = 60\% $$
    Revenue = 60% × 750,000 = RM450,000.
